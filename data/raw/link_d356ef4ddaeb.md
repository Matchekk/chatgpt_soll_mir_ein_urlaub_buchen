Unterkunftsinformationen
Villajoyosa is a beautiful fishing village famous for its beautiful facade of brightly painted houses, which were once the homes of its fishermen, and which together with a promenade, form one of the most beautiful and photographed postcards of the Costa Blanca. It has one of the best preserved historic quarters on the Valencian coast. It begins to make out the peaks of the Sierras de Aitana and Puig Campana, a barrier that defends the region from the north winds and allows you to enjoy very pleasant temperatures all year round and numerous hiking routes for mountain lovers.
Informationen zur Gegend
Located between El Campello and Benidorm, La Vila Joiosa defends another type of tourism, more discreet and relaxed. The 15 kilometers of coastline of its municipal area and its more than ten beaches, the character of its privileged inhabitants and its model of responsible urbanism make this traditional fishing village the counterpoint to the bustle of other nearby towns.
